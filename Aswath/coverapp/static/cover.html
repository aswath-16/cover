ABOUT THE BOOK

This book "COMPUTER NETWORKS" provides a detailed and easy-to-understand explanation of how computer networks work. It covers network layers, protocols, routing, switching, wireless communication, network security, and real-world internet architecture. Tanenbaum uses simple examples and diagrams to explain complex concepts, making it perfect for beginners as well as advanced learners who want a strong foundation in networking.

A network is powerful not because of wires and routers, but because it connects people, ideas, and possibilities
